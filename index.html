About

The Fall of Hymelhan is a vast, open world adventuremap, focused on exploration, adventure, and the discovery of ancient histories. It is one of the largest Minecraft maps ever produced, both in block count and in hours invested.


Although Hymelhan is still under development, and will stay as so for the foreseeable future, what parts of it that have already been crafted are detailed unlike any other map. Thousands of hours have already been put towards shaping and perfecting it's full 15kx15k breadth, almost transforming it into an entire game within another.
